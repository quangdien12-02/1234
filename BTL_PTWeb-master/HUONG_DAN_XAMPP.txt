Huong dan chay project tren XAMPP

1. Copy thu muc BTL_PTWeb-master vao:
   C:\xampp\htdocs\

2. Mo XAMPP Control Panel, bat Apache va MySQL.

3. Mo trinh duyet vao:
   http://localhost/phpmyadmin

4. Chon tab Import, chon file db_ptweb.sql trong thu muc project, bam Go.

5. Chay website:
   http://localhost/BTL_PTWeb-master/

6. Dang nhap tai khoan quan tri:
   Ma sinh vien / ma quan tri: ADMIN
   Ten dang nhap: admin
   Mat khau: 123456

Chuc nang da co:
- Xem danh sach sinh vien
- Tim kiem sinh vien
- Them sinh vien
- Sua thong tin sinh vien
- Xoa sinh vien
